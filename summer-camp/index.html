I.N.S.P.I.R.E Series: Creative Innovator Workshop (Summer Camp 2k24)

Workshop Highlights

Join our transformative "I.N.S.P.I.R.E Series" workshop for kids and ladies, a 5-day journey fostering creativity, innovation, and personal growth. Designed for different age groups, including Junior Creators (ages 10-15), Creative Dynamo (ages 16-25), and Leadership Catalysts (ages 18-35, exclusively for women). Through interactive sessions, hands-on activities, and expert-guided exploration, discover your creative potential, enhance innovation skills, and ignite a passion for peak performance.
